# Multi-Account Feature - Quick Start Guide

## ✅ Already Integrated and Ready!

The multi-account feature is **fully implemented** and **integrated** into your app. No additional setup needed!

## How to Use

### 1. Login with Your First Account
- Open the app
- Login with Google, GitHub, or Email
- Your account is automatically saved

### 2. Look at Profile Navigation (Bottom of Sidebar)
You'll see your profile picture with a **+ icon** in the top-right corner:
```
┌─────────┐
│  [👤]   │
│    +    │  ← Click this to add another account
└─────────┘
```

### 3. Add Another Account
- Click the **+ icon**
- You'll be redirected to the login page
- Login with a different account (different email/provider)
- The new account is added

### 4. Notice the Icon Changed
After adding a second account, the icon changes to **^** (chevron up):
```
┌─────────┐
│  [👤]   │
│    ^    │  ← Click this to see all accounts
└─────────┘
```

### 5. Open the Account Dropdown
- Click the **^ icon**
- A dropdown appears showing all your accounts:
```
┌──────────────────────────────┐
│ [👤] John Doe            ✓  │ ← Your current account
│      john@example.com        │
│                              │
│ [👤] Jane Smith              │ ← Other account
│      jane@example.com        │
├──────────────────────────────┤
│ [+]  Add account             │ ← Add more accounts
└──────────────────────────────┘
```

### 6. Switch Between Accounts
- Click on any account in the dropdown
- You'll be logged out of the current account
- Redirected to login page with email pre-filled
- Enter password and login
- You're now using that account!

### 7. Add More Accounts
- Click the **^ icon** to open dropdown
- Click **"Add account"** at the bottom
- Login with another account
- It's added to your list

## That's It!

The feature is ready to use right now. Just:
1. Login
2. Look for the + icon on your profile
3. Click it to add accounts
4. Click ^ to switch between them

## Visual Guide

### Where to Find It
The account switcher is located on your **profile button** in the **sidebar navigation** (bottom of the sidebar).

### Icon States
- **+ icon** = Single account (click to add more)
- **^ icon** = Multiple accounts (click to see dropdown)

### Dropdown Features
- Shows all logged-in accounts
- Active account has a checkmark (✓)
- Click any account to switch
- Click "Add account" to add more

## Tips

- You can have unlimited accounts
- Each account has its own chats, settings, and data
- Switching is fast and secure
- Accounts are saved on your device
- No need to logout/login repeatedly
